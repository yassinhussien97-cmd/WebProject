civil engineering

Civil engineering is the branch of engineering that deals with the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure.

Real-Life Applications:

  1. Constructing skyscrapers
  2. Building highways and tunnels
  3. Designing drainage systems

important skills:

  1. AutoCAD and design software
  2. Mathematics and physics
  3. Problem-solving skills

Main Fields:

  • Structural engineering (buildings, bridges)
  • Transportation engineering (roads, railways)
  • Environmental engineering (water systems)
  • Geotechnical engineering (soil and foundations)

Significant characters:

    • Gustave Eiffel
    • Isaac Newton
    • John Smeaton
  2. ARAB

important skills:

  2. Technical problem-solving
  3. Process design

Main Fields:

  • Control systems
  • Electronics
  • Renewable energy
